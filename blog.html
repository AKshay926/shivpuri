Taste of Shivpuri

Taste of Shivpuri

When you are in our city you will observe diffrent kind of tasty snacks and delicious cusine which will make your stay more pleasent here. The Streets are filled with these snacks from morning till late nights.

Let's look at some snacks and Cafes which will make you more foodie.

Chaat

Feel the taste of chaat with some handmade spices. You can easily get roadside tracks from stalls or food carts across the city.

Poha

Well we all know that when you are in Madhya Pradesh, Poha won't leave from your list. Our all time favourite breakfast.

Bedmi Poori (बेडई)

When you come across the north belt of Madhya pradesh this is the must have breakfast for you. You will observe a long que on the shops where bedmi poori is found.

Samosa

You might have took taste of this snack at various places but we assure you after having samosa in shivpuri you will feel the taste of handmade spices.

Cafes in Shivpuri

Chai Sutta Bar

The Chain of Chai Sutta Bar is all over India and we also have one where you can enjoy your snacks with Evening tea.

Food and Roll

A perfect cafe for you if you want some diffrent cusines as they provide you with diffrent kind of tastes.

Hug in Hearts (H2)

One of the finest cafe of our city where you can enjoy snacks and main course too.

MP 33 cafe

MP 33 cafe is located in the heart of city where you will find all kinds of snacks and dishes.
